МРЕЖА is an interdisciplinary architecture-based platform which has a tendency to promote and present the multilayered nature and complexity of architectural practice, and also to offer wider engagement in this on-going project. The idea of connecting people of different profiles spans the intertwining of different art genres, technology and development in other areas of art production. Beside from being an intersection of contemporary artistic practise, МРЕЖА aims to inspire further creative process of art production.
